Terrifio™ Digital IP Cost Forecaster

About:

The Terrifio™ Digital IP Cost Forecaster gives you a general idea of what the average total costs might be for your Project to :

  1. file a first patent application;
  2. have the application examined by a Patent Office Examiner; and
  3. have the application successfully result in a patent;

    4. Choose a “Country of Filing”, and indicate a “Complexity”.

    Also indicate a “Proximity to Prior Art”, from “Nothing even close” to “Lots of earlier similar examples”

    These cost forecasts are general in nature and may not accurately reflect the costs your Project. An IP Professional may determine that other costs may apply, and/or that a forecast may be too low or too high. For example, the process to filing a patent application on an urgent basis, to meet a particular deadline, can sometimes raise costs. In other cases, costs can vary quite significantly based on an entity status in a particular patent office. For instance, in the United States, there are different rates depending whether an application is a Large Entity or Small Entity.

    The Digital IP Cost Forecaster also projects a grant date, which is a general determination based on broad average pendency times. Specific times for examination of a patent application can vary considerably. For instance, in some countries, and some conditions, examination can be accelerated on payment of an additional fee or by relying on the past examination of a corresponding application in another country.

Using the country drop down list, The Terrifio™ Cost Forecaster gives you a visual representation of potential costs you might see when securing your IP in several national jurisdictions.
